Assessment Details

Assessment 1 – Initial Proposal

info Summary

Grade Grade 10
Project Sustainable Future Cities
Assessment Type Presentation
After Challenge #3
Work Form Group / Individual

description Student Output Requirements

The initial proposal should outline the core sustainable technologies chosen for the city model. Students must present a 5-minute pitch covering energy, waste, and water management systems. The output should include visual aids (sketches or digital mockups) and a clear justification for why these technologies were selected based on environmental impact and feasibility.

Competencies Assessed in This Assessment

Competency Description Weight Target
[SP1.C3] Critical Thinking
Analyzes sustainable options and justifies choices based on research data. Primary Individual
[SP1.C5] Communication
Delivers a clear, persuasive pitch with professional visual support. Primary Individual
[SP2.C1] Collaboration
Demonstrates shared responsibility in developing the city concept. Secondary Group
[SP3.C2] Global Citizenship
Identifies environmental impacts on a global scale through local action. Secondary Group
[SP1.C1] Creativity
Proposes novel solutions for urban infrastructure and waste. Secondary Group

assignment_ind Additional Instructions for Teacher

Use the following guidelines to ensure consistency across different student groups during the scoring phase.

Ensure that each student speaks for at least 60 seconds during the group pitch to accurately assess individual communication skills. If a student is absent, evaluate their contribution through the written part of the proposal.
